Elemental Bolt
Tier 1 - Active Ability
Cost: 2 Action Points
Range: 60-Feet (12 Sq)
To-Hit: 1d20 + ARC
Damage: 1d6 + ARC
Description
Fire a bolt of elemental energy of your choice at a target. (Fire, Ice, Lightning)
Upgrades
Charged Power
You can choose to spend 3 Action Points to have it deal 1d10 + ARC damage instead.
Arcing Bolt
When firing Elemental Bolt, you may now choose an additional target within 10 feet of the original. The additional target takes damage equal to your ARC modifier.
Connections
RP Feat -> Magic Restoration
RP Feat -> Control Elements
RP Feat -> Telekenisis
